Classic Margherita Pizza

How to bake this art

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust or italian bread shell
  • 3 plum tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1/3 cup pesto with basil
  • 1.5 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 230.C. Place pizza crust on baking sheet
  2. Spread pesto over pizza crust. Arrange tomatoes over pesto. Sprinkle with cheese and crushed red pepper
  3. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown. Cut into wedges

And there you go, enjoy your meal
